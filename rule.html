Rules and regulations

For proper code of conduct of all the fest and events, the lead has designed some rules and regulations.They are as follows:

  1. All the paricipantes must ensure that they have registered for their respective events.
  2. The decision of judge is final and binding
  3. Use of unfair means shall lead to immediate disqualification of the participants
  4. Participants are expected to be respectful towards everyone.
  5. All the paricipantes must be present in the opening ceremony.
  6. All the paricipantes must be present in the closing ceremony where certificates will be destributed.
